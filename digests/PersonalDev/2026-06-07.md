## 📺 Daniel Pink

### [Talk to You 10 Years from Now!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jgAndKYEQnU)
*Published: June 7, 2026*

Pink challenges you to imagine your future self — ten years out — and ask whether you'd be able to justify today's inaction to that person. The framing reframes procrastination not as laziness but as a failure of accountability to your own future. A sharp nudge for anyone sitting on a decision they've been avoiding.

---

### [The Second Score](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U7BA8PPD4Fw)
*Published: June 7, 2026*

Pink introduces the concept of the "second score" — how you respond to a bad result matters as much as the result itself. The idea is that resilience and professionalism are visible and measurable, separate from raw performance. Useful mental model for anyone navigating feedback or setbacks at work.
