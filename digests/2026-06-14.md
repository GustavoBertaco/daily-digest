## 🌐 Uber Engineering

**[Building Uber's Multi-Cloud Secrets Management Platform to Enhance Security](https://www.uber.com/blog/building-ubers-multi-cloud-secrets-management-platform/)**
*Published: June 13, 2026*

- Uber operates over 5,000 microservices, 5,000 databases, and 500,000+ analytical jobs per day, secured by more than 150,000 secrets that authenticate across distributed systems including 400 third-party vendor integrations and 400 SaaS applications.
- The scale of that secrets footprint makes centralized, multi-cloud secrets management a hard security requirement rather than a nice-to-have.
- The post frames rising cyberattacks as the forcing function for building a dedicated platform rather than relying on ad-hoc credential management.

**[Accelerating Search and Ingestion with High-Performance gRPC™ in OpenSearch™](https://www.uber.com/blog/high-performance-grpc/)**
*Published: June 13, 2026*

- Search at Uber underpins nearly every real-time experience — rider-driver matching, fraud detection, Eats recommendations — and as traffic, payload size, and latency sensitivity grew, REST/JSON became not just slower but a structural constraint on how the search platform could scale.
- The post traces the architectural response to that ceiling: adopting high-performance gRPC in OpenSearch to lift the constraints REST/JSON imposed on the search platform.
- The focus covers both search and ingestion, reflecting gRPC's role across the full data path in OpenSearch.

**[Evolution and Scale of Uber's Delivery Search Platform](https://www.uber.com/blog/evolution-and-scale-of-ubers-delivery-search-platform/)**
*Published: June 13, 2026*

- Search is the primary discovery funnel for Uber Eats — a large share of orders begin at the search bar — meaning search quality directly translates into conversion, basket quality, and time-to-order, especially for long-tail queries, new or seasonal items, and multilingual markets.
- When search misses intent, people bounce or fall back to browsing.
- The post documents how the delivery search platform evolved to meet that demand at scale.
